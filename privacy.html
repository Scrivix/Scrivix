Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. Scrivix is designed from the ground up to protect your data and give you complete peace of mind during your live presentations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect absolutely zero personal data. Scrivix does not require user accounts, logins, tracking cookies, or email registration to use the core application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your drawing inputs, presentation content, or screen data.

2. Local Network Communication

The companion iPad application communicates with the macOS server application exclusively over your local local area network (Wi-Fi or direct peer-to-peer connection). No data payload ever leaves your local network or transits through external cloud servers. Your presentation remains entirely private to your physical space.

3. Third-Party Services

Because we do not track analytics or use external cloud platforms, no data is shared with third-party advertising companies or data brokers.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us via our support channels.

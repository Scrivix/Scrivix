Scrivix Tool Guide

A quick reference for the iPad toolbar and Mac interface.

iPad Toolbar Options

Icon Tool & Description
📸 Screenshot Snapshot

Takes a high-quality capture of your current slide with all annotations included and saves it to your iPad.
🎨 Color Palette

Quickly switch between black, red, blue, green, orange, and purple inks for your active drawing tool.
🖥️ Whiteboard Mode

Toggles a solid background over your screen, giving you a clean slate to write out complex ideas without slide clutter.
💧 Pressure Sensitivity

Toggles Apple Pencil pressure and tilt sensitivity for more dynamic, natural-looking strokes.
🎇 Laser Pointer

Draw temporary strokes that slowly fade away. Perfect for highlighting attention without leaving a permanent mark.
🧼 Eraser

Removes individual strokes that you swipe over with your Pencil or finger.
🧹 Clear Screen

Instantly wipes all current annotations from the screen so you can start fresh on the next slide.
🖱️ Interaction Mode

Temporarily hides the drawing layer and turns your iPad into a trackpad so you can click through your Mac presentation.
✍️ Draw Toggle

The master switch to turn the drawing overlay on or off.
↩️ Undo / Redo

Step backward or forward through your recent pen strokes.
🎚️ Brush Size Slider

Drag left to make your pen thinner, or right to make it thicker for bold visibility.
❌ Disconnect

Safely ends the wireless session with your Mac.

Mac Application Icons

Icon Tool & Description
📡 Menu Bar Status

Scrivix lives in your Mac's top menu bar. Click the icon to view your current connection status.
⚙️ Preferences

Access network settings, adjust presentation display bounds, and manage app permissions.

# Scrivix

Turn your iPad and Apple Pencil into a wireless annotation and control surface for your Mac.

Scrivix lets you draw, highlight, point, scroll, click, and control your Mac during live lessons, meetings, screen recordings, and presentations. It is built for educators, presenters, trainers, and creators who want the precision of iPad input with the flexibility of a Mac desktop.

## What Scrivix Does

- Annotate over Keynote, PowerPoint, videos, browsers, PDFs, documents, and other Mac apps.
- Use your iPad and Apple Pencil as a wireless drawing surface for your Mac.
- Control your Mac from iPad with tap, double-click, right-click, tap-drag, hover movement, and two-finger scrolling.
- Switch between transparent overlay mode, whiteboard backgrounds, graph paper, lined paper, solid colors, and photo backgrounds.
- Use presentation tools including Brush, Highlighter, Laser Pointer, Fill, Pixel Eraser, Stroke Eraser, Undo, Redo, and clean screenshots.
- Pair locally using a secure 4-digit passcode.

## Privacy First

Scrivix works over your local network. Your drawings, screen stream, and control input are not routed through Scrivix cloud servers.

No account is required. No subscription is required.

## Requirements

### iPad

- iPadOS 17.0 or later
- Optimized for Apple Pencil Pro, Apple Pencil 2nd generation, and Apple Pencil USB-C

### Mac Companion

- macOS Ventura, Sonoma, or Sequoia
- Apple silicon Mac recommended
- Screen Recording permission
- Accessibility / Computer Control permission for Control Mode
